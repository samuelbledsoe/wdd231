Grocery Hacks

  • Buy protein in bulk (chicken thighs, eggs, beans) and freeze portions.
  • Use unit pricing to compare value across sizes and brands.
  • Plan 2–3 base recipes and remix with sauces/spices.
  • Choose frozen vegetables over fresh to reduce waste.
