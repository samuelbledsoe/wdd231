Budget Muscle Meals

A site plan for the individual website project

Site Name

Budget Muscle Meals

This name was chosen because it clearly represents the target audience—people who want to build muscle but are on a tight food budget. It is easy to remember and directly communicates the purpose of the site.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide weekly meal plans, grocery shopping tips, and affordable, high-protein recipes for people focused on fitness and muscle-building without spending a lot of money. This site will serve as a practical resource for budget-conscious fitness enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • What are some cheap, high-protein meals I can prep for the week?
  • How can I build muscle while spending less than $75 per week on groceries?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue) — used for headers and navigation background.
  • Accent Color: #27AE60 (Strong Green) — used for call-to-action buttons and highlights.
  • Background: #FFFFFF (White) — general page background.
  • Text: #333333 — used for body content text.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Bebas Neue — bold and modern, used for page titles and section headings.
  • Body Font: Open Sans — clean and readable, used for paragraphs and list content.
