About Me

Hi, I’m Sam Bledsoe—a web developer in training with a passion for clean design, smart code, and meaningful purpose. I’m currently studying software development through BYU-Idaho’s online program, where I’ve been building skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. What drives me is the vision of a future where I can blend creativity with code to build tools and sites that help real people solve real problems. I’m especially interested in front-end development, UX, and meaningful tech—projects that make life easier, better, or more beautiful. While I have big career goals, my greatest motivation comes from home. I’m a proud husband and dad, and my family is at the center of everything I do. Every late-night study session, every line of code—it’s all building toward a life of stability, adventure, and purpose for them. Thanks for stopping by my page!
